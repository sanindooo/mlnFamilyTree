KOSAYI WAGWA
 He was the 6th son and 13th child of Katikkiro Martin Luther Nsibirwa.
 He was born in 1927.
 His mother was called Naome Kipnade.
 He went to Kings College Buddo for his junior and senior education and later to Bukalasa Agricultural
College in Wobulenzi-Luwero. 
 In the late 50s, he settled in Kiti Village – Ggomobola Nangabo – Ssaaza Kyadondo. He was mainly in 
the transport business between Kasangati and Matugga.
 Due to gorilla wars of the early 80s; he later on moved to Namakomo to manage the Greater Nsibirwa 
family farm where he stayed until he died in 1998 at the age of 71 years from prostate cancer.
 He had a gift of being so neat, which was a well-known fact by all his siblings, and he was strict with 
children as well.
